Instructions

How to play:

You must find the item in your card that matches the card in the discard deck. Then you have to click on it.
You must try to find so many items as you can in the time given
With a right-click you can find the words and image of the item if you dont know the word

Options

See the words in your current deck:

Click in the "Words" button and you can see all the words in the current deck including their pictures
