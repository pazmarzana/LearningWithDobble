Instructions

How to play:

To start the game click on the "Play" button.
You must find and click on the item in your card (right deck) that matches one item in the discarded card (left deck).
If you get it right you win 10 points and your card goes to the discard deck, if you make a mistake you lose 5 points.
You must discard as many cards as you can in the given time.
If you dont know the word/meaning of an item you can find it with a right-click on it (or in mobile long press).

Options

See the words in your current deck:

Click in the "Vocabulary" button and you will see all the words in the current deck including their pictures.
