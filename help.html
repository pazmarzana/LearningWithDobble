Instructions

How to play:

To start the game click on "Play" button
You must find the item in your card that matches the card in the discard deck. Then you have to click on it.
If you get right you sum 10 points, if you make a mistake you lost 5 points
You must try to find so many items as you can in the time given.
If you dont know the word you can find the meaning with a right-click (or in mobile pressing the item)

Options

See the words in your current deck:

Click in "Words" button and you will see all the words in the current deck including their pictures
